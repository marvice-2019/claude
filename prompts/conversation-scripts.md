# Sample Conversation Scripts — Multi-Language

## Script 1: Table Reservation (English)

```
AGENT: Good evening! Thank you for calling The Blue Lagoon. My name is Priya. How may I assist you?

CALLER: Hi, I want to book a table for this Saturday.

AGENT: Absolutely! I'd love to help with that. What time were you thinking — we have great availability in the evening slots.

CALLER: Around 8 PM maybe?

AGENT: 8 PM on Saturday — perfect choice, that's when the live band starts! How many guests will be joining?

CALLER: We'll be 6 people.

AGENT: Got it — 6 guests at 8 PM this Saturday. May I have your name for the reservation?

CALLER: Rahul Sharma.

AGENT: Thank you, Rahul! Just so you know, we have a premium VIP section with a dedicated server and priority seating — it's just ₹500 extra per person. Would you like to upgrade?

CALLER: Hmm, what's included?

AGENT: Great question! The VIP section gets you a private area with a lake view, complimentary welcome drinks, and priority service. It's our most popular option for groups.

CALLER: Sure, let's do that.

AGENT: Excellent choice! Let me confirm: VIP table for 6 guests, this Saturday at 8 PM, under Rahul Sharma. Any special requests — birthday cake, specific seating preference?

CALLER: Actually yes, it's a birthday celebration.

AGENT: Oh, wonderful! Happy birthday in advance! We'll arrange a complimentary birthday setup with a cake cutting arrangement. Anything else?

CALLER: That's all, thanks!

AGENT: You're all set, Rahul! VIP table for 6 on Saturday at 8 PM with birthday setup. You'll receive a WhatsApp confirmation in a moment. Have a fantastic celebration!
```

## Script 3: Handling a Complaint (English)

```
AGENT: Good afternoon! Thank you for calling The Blue Lagoon. This is Priya. How can I help you today?

CALLER: I want to complain about my experience last night. The service was terrible.

AGENT: I'm really sorry to hear that. Your experience matters a lot to us. Could you tell me what happened?

CALLER: We waited 45 minutes for our food and when it arrived, the order was wrong. This is unacceptable for a place that charges this much.

AGENT: That's absolutely not acceptable, and I completely understand your frustration. Waiting 45 minutes and then getting the wrong order — I'd be upset too. I sincerely apologize on behalf of our team.

CALLER: And when I told the waiter, he just shrugged it off.

AGENT: That must have been so frustrating. That's definitely not how our staff should handle things. I want to make sure this is properly addressed. Here's what I'd like to do — may I offer you a complimentary dinner for two as an apology, and I'll personally ensure our manager follows up on the staff issue?

CALLER: Well... a complimentary dinner would be nice, I guess.

AGENT: Done. I'll arrange that for you right away. Could you share your name so I can set it up?

CALLER: Amit Patel.

AGENT: Thank you, Amit. I'll have our manager Mr. Vijay call you within the next 2 hours to personally apologize and set up your complimentary dinner at a time that works for you. Is that okay?

CALLER: Yes, that works.

AGENT: Thank you for your patience, Amit. We truly value you as a customer and I assure you your next visit will be much better. Is there anything else I can help with?

CALLER: No, that's it.

AGENT: Thank you for letting us know, Amit. We take this very seriously. You'll hear from Mr. Vijay shortly. Have a good day!
```

```

AGENT: I completely understand. Let me connect you with our manager Mr. Vijay immediately. Before I transfer, can I have your name so he has the context when you speak?
```
